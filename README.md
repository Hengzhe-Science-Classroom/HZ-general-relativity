# General Relativity · Interactive

**Spacetime, curvature, and gravitation**

**Status:** Planned

Part of [Hengzhe's Science Classroom](https://hengzhe-science-classroom.github.io/hub/) · Tier 5 · Research Frontier

## What You'll Learn

- Special relativity and tensor algebra
- Manifolds and curvature
- Einstein's field equations
- The Schwarzschild solution and black holes
- Gravitational waves
- Cosmological models: FRW metric, dark energy

## References

- Sean M. Carroll, *Spacetime and Geometry: An Introduction to General Relativity* (Cambridge)
- Robert M. Wald, *General Relativity* (University of Chicago Press)
- Charles W. Misner, Kip S. Thorne & John A. Wheeler, *Gravitation* (Princeton)

## Features (when built)

- **Interactive visualizations** — every concept comes alive with draggable, animated demos
- **KaTeX math rendering** — rigorous notation throughout
- **Exercises with hints & solutions** — from computational to proof-based
- **Progress tracking** — saved locally in your browser
- **Zero dependencies** — just open the link and learn
